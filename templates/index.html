Analysis

If user has uploaded a file, the data will provide the following results:

  • Precision: The ratio of correctly predicted positive observations to the total predicted positives.
  • Recall (Sensitivity): The ratio of correctly predicted positive observations to all actual positives.
  • F1-Score: The weighted average of Precision and Recall.

These metrics together give you a comprehensive view of how effectively our model is analyzing your data. Our goal is to provide you with reliable and actionable insights, empowering you to make informed decisions based on the results.
